About

Blockchain technology is considered to be a game-changer for decentralizing infrastructure and building a trust layer for business logic. It is envisioned to be a technology that could propel us into the next industrial revolution, with new paradigms for doing business in the finance, supply chain, transportation, shared economy, and many other industries.

In the supply chain industry, blockchain creates visibility and efficiency among complex and oftentimes opaque processes. From fraud reduction to insurance claims, paperwork automation to auditing, and real-time container tracking to optimizing service and delivery, the ways in which blockchain can shape this industry are endless.

Join us for a two day exploration of this revolutionary technology in supply chain and logistics. You will meet with experts from supply chain and blockchain, discover the problems facing the industry and the solutions being developed, and leave with the tools needed to transform your business processes with blockchain.
