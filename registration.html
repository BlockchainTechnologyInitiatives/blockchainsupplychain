Travel Arrangements

The conference will take place on May 21-22, 2018 at the Hyatt Regency Houston Intercontinental Airport hotel. The address of the Hyatt is 425 North Sam Houston Pkwy E. Houston, TX 77060.

You can fly in to George Bush Intercontinental Airport, which is about two miles northeast of the venue, or William P. Hobby Airport about twenty miles south of the venue. Visit fly2houston.com for more information about flights.

Rooms are available at the Hyatt at a discounted rate if you reserve here. There are many nearby hotels as well:
